Chemistry 1-2

Chemistry AP

Description: Chemistry AP is a second year class. It is a college-level course designed to teach the concepts covered in the AP course outline and prepare the students to pass the AP exam. Math Analysis is required.

Text: Chemistry, Ninth Edition, Chang, McGraw Hill, 2007.
